ESNS
Emergency School Notification System






About Us
ESNS is meant to be used by students and administrators alike to have the ability to report and track a live shooter. It is also meant to be able to receive live updates and allow special administration.

Every user must give basic information for student and admin such as: name, school e-mail, phone number. This will allow the website to push notifications. The server and database must be able to pass location information.
Features





Police Assist

If the student is safe, our application provides the ability for students to report where an active shooter is. This information is then passed onto police to help them locate the suspects as quickly as possible.

Find Loved Ones

We provide the ability to track your loved ones location and safety, live, without fear of overloading the cell networks.

Admin Dashboard

Our advanced administrative dashboard provides police and school administrators up-to-the-second reports and status of the emergency. Live tracking of all students and suspects on a map.

Speed and Reliability

During an emergency, our website goes into low-bandwidth, high-performance mode, ensuring that cell and WiFi networks remain online and not overloaded. Our servers can handle hundreds of thousands of requests per second.
